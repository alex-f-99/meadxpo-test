Our Exclusive Traits

Medaxpo aims to provide world class infrastructure to revolutionize the way virtual events are conducted. It has reformed the virtual event space by incorporating the following exclusive traits:

Customised Registration forms

Registration forms could be modified to include fields that the service provider wishes to know about their participant. This helps to collect data about their audience.

Event Halls

Event halls are similar to physical stalls in a virtual context. It also incorporates the feature of meeting rooms.

Guidance to audience

Visitors are welcomed in each virtual lobby with information desks available to guide them to their respective stall.

Booth

Booths are specially structured tool in which the visitors can interact. It has the capabilities to host images, videos, brochures, data sheet, etc that bring virtual objects to life.

Virtual Briefcase

It is a kind of folder that keeps a track of all the items where you show your interests and makes it available for download after the event.

Interaction

Medaxpo has an environment where the audience and the host can interact on a real time basis through text, video, or audio.

Attractions

It allows host to speak during the event and conduct polls and take questions during the event.

Customised reports

It auto populates the results of the events providing the statistics of participants who turned up and how the event performed.
